What We Do

Clothing is just the beginning.

Pass It On runs a suite of community programmes that turn idle garments into a movement of dignity and care across Kenya.

👗

Clothing Redistribution

We collect gently-used clothing from households and businesses, sort and clean it, then deliver it with dignity to children's homes, correctional facilities, and underserved families.

🧺

Essentials Support

Beyond clothing, we provide basic needs support including food parcels and hygiene essentials for our most vulnerable beneficiaries during outreach visits.

🤝

Community Outreach

Our volunteer teams conduct regular visits to orphanages, prisons, and community centres, building lasting relationships rooted in compassion and accountability.

♻️

Sustainability Awareness

We educate communities about circular fashion, responsible consumption, and the environmental impact of textile waste, inspiring a new generation of conscious givers.

How It Works

Three steps. Infinite dignity.

Our structured model ensures every donation reaches the right hands with full transparency and care.

1

Collection

Community drives, drop-off bins, and online coordination make donating simple and accessible from anywhere in Kenya.

2

Processing

Every item is sorted, cleaned, and carefully categorised by our volunteer teams to ensure quality and suitability for each beneficiary.

3

Delivery

Coordinated drop-offs to verified institutions, complete with storytelling, feedback, and documentation to strengthen accountability.
